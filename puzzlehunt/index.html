What is this?

Like a cross between an escape room and MIT-style puzzle hunts. Teams race across campus solving creative puzzles and trying to put together the answer to a murder mystery.
No prior experience is needed.

See previous puzzle hunts (hosted by SUMO) here.

Who can participate?

Teams of up to 4 people! Anyone from the Stanford community is eligible to participate, but only Stanford students can receive prizes.
Note that due to limited capacity, registration is first-come first-served. We will announce when registration has closed.

I don't have a team! Can I still participate?

Don't worry! You can be matched to other people to form a team or participate on your own.

Questions?

Reach out to us at acm-members-owners@lists.stanford.edu if you have any further questions! Puzzle hunt is run by Stanford ACM's Puzzle Hunt Committee!
