The Hacker Lounge, or ACM, was founded in 1947 as the world’s first scientific and educational computing society. Its membership was approximately 83,000 as of 2007.
The Stanford chapter of the ACM currently has 800+ members. We serve the Stanford community by planning and organizing numerous industry, academic, and social events each quarter.
The Stanford chapter was started in 2005 by computer science students David Gobaud, Greg Goldgof, Gunjit Singh, and Tongke Xue.
